Online Store - Daintree

"Online store with paypal payments intergration and guest user shopping."


An e-commerce website crafted with passion and powered by the Django web framework and Django Rest Framework. It's designed to provide users with a secure online shopping experience.
I've prioritized user authentication and authorization to ensure that every interaction with the platform is both personalized and safe. Users can easily register, log in, and manage their profiles, all while knowing their data is protected. Our extensive product catalog showcases a diverse range of products, and each item comes with a detailed product page to help shoppers make informed decisions.
One of the features of Daintree is its shopping cart system. Shoppers can add products to their cart, update quantities as needed, and when ready, proceed to a secure checkout process. Also with PayPal integration, users can complete their transactions confidently and conveniently. The admin panel simplifies product management, order tracking, and user account administration. It's a powerful tool to keep everything running smoothly.



Features ::

  • User Authentication and Authorization
  • Product Catalog
  • Product Details
  • Search Functionality
  • Customer Reviews
  • Shopping Cart
  • Secure Checkout
  • Order Placement
  • User Profiles and Account Settings
  • Admin Panel
